06 — How to Brew

Get the most from every bean.

Four methods, four moods. Each tested in our Surat studio.

01

French Press

4 min · Full body

  1. 30g coarse ground (KIYO Blend works beautifully)
  2. 500ml water at 93°C
  3. Stir, lid on, bloom for 4 minutes
  4. Press slowly. Pour. Drink within 5 minutes.
02

Pour Over (V60)

3 min · Clean cup

  1. 20g medium-fine ground (try YUHI Blend)
  2. Rinse filter, discard water
  3. 30s bloom with 60ml water
  4. Pour in spirals to 320ml total
03

Moka Pot

5 min · Espresso-like

  1. 18g fine ground (YOKO Blend recommended)
  2. Fill base to safety valve with hot water
  3. Medium heat, lid open
  4. Pull off heat as soon as it gurgles
04

AeroPress

2 min · Clean & punchy

  1. 15g medium-fine ground (YUHI Blend shines here)
  2. Inverted method: 220ml water at 85°C
  3. Stir, cap, wait 90 seconds
  4. Flip onto cup, press slowly for ~30 seconds

11 — FAQ

Things people ask us.

How fresh are the beans when they arrive?

Every bag is packed close to shipping date. We don't keep large inventories — what you get is as fresh as we can make it.

Whole bean or ground?

Both are available — leave us a note on WhatsApp or in your order. If you want ground, tell us your brew method (French press, pour-over, moka, espresso) and we'll grind accordingly.

How do I store coffee at home?

Airtight container, away from light, heat, and moisture. Skip the fridge — that introduces humidity. Whole beans stay fresh ~3 weeks after roasting; ground coffee, ~2 weeks.

How does WhatsApp ordering work?

Click any "Order on WhatsApp" button, or message +91 95743 23011. Send us your cart, address, and preferred payment. We confirm, dispatch, and share tracking — usually within an hour.

Where do you deliver?

We currently deliver only in Surat. Delivery is free within a 5 km radius of Piplod (covers Piplod, Vesu, Citylight, Athwa). Other Surat pincodes: a small ₹50 delivery fee. Outside Surat? Message us on WhatsApp — we can arrange a courier and confirm the cost with you before dispatch.

Do you offer wholesale or cafe partnerships?

Yes. We supply over a dozen independent cafes across Gujarat. Drop us a WhatsApp message with your venue and volume needs — we'll send pricing within the day.

Is your cold brew sweetened?

Never. Just water and coffee — no sugar, no preservatives. Sweetness comes naturally from the slow extraction. Keep refrigerated and consume within 7 days of opening.

What if something arrives wrong or damaged?

Just call or message us. We're a small team in Surat — one quick WhatsApp or phone call and we'll make it right. No forms, no fuss.
