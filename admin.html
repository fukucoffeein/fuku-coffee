Full reset — Go Live 🚀 Deletes all orders, chats, stock batches & history, and sets every product's stock to 0. Then add your real stock via Stock → Add Stock.

Permissions

🛡️ You're a standard admin. The Super Admin (Aakash) handles destructive actions: full reset, resellers, partner investments & equity, products.
  • ✅ View dashboard, sales reports, charts
  • ✅ Manage stock — add batches, adjust, edit
  • ✅ Manage orders — view, edit, update status
  • ✅ Confirm payments (Nisarg)
  • ✅ Manage subscriptions
  • ✅ View team, chatbot logs, resellers
  • 🛡️ Super only: add/edit resellers
  • 🛡️ Super only: edit partner investments & equity
  • 🛡️ Super only: create/delete products
  • 🛡️ Super only: reset / wipe data
